Poomsae:

  • Ahn makki preset is head height
  • Horse-riding stance is shoulder width. ... maybe?... It’s also deep - a lot of bend at the knee
  • ahn makki preset is at 135° from a birds eye view
  • choombi is a five count

  • Taegeuk 4 (sa jang): 
  • At the end the ahn makki punches (2), and ahn makki double punches (2) are fast
  • Push block is at 135° from a birds eye view
  • Push block is further out from the body - arm at about 160°

  • In jepic poom mok chigi, the neck strike arm is straight
  • Taegeuk 5: (o jang)
  • the preset and the hammer fist sidekick are fast - just as fast as a normal sidekick preset
  • The first two moves (down block hammer fist) are consecutive/fast. Hammer fist is fast
  • The elbow strike after han sonnal makki is at the chin
  • On the final move there is no jump, just a stomp
  • Taegeuk 7 (chil jang):
  • in the back sequence wait to open the hand until the crescent kick is just about to hit it- keep the hammerfist closed while you raise the kick
  • Koryo:
  • in the back sequence after the time break, negative hands are straight and fully extended (Haylee Ann was right)
  • Knee break- thumb is up
  • Time break is an eight count
  • Keumgang:
  • hakdari is an eight count (very slow)
  • First forward sequence of Keumgang is fast and neck strikes are at 45°
  • Taebaek:
  • The backfist to temple has a 90 degree bend at elbow

Kicks:

  • Front kick:
  • don’t round back
  • Straighten bottom leg
  • Bring knee up as high as possible before kicking
  • Hook kick:
  • turn upper body opposite on kick
  • Chest over knee on kick
  • Tornado:
  • bring hand opposite on kick
  • Throw first knee diagonal
  • Flying side kick:
  • Upper body twists opposite on kick
  • Bring leg up at same time as kick
  • Throw both arms up on jump
  • 540 hook:
  • wait to jump until first knee is past target
  • Throw first knee diagonal
  • Second kick is just turning knee over
  • On final kick, turn shoulder and arms opposite like a hook kick
  • Land with chest over knee

Arm motion for tornado and 540:

  • Bring arms up but together, like you're throwing a bucket of water. Then in to tuck.

Emily's other notes:

  • Land safely by bending knee and going to front stance, for all kicks. Also land with your chest over your leg.
  • Start kicks in wide stance. Don't step in to a short stance. It will make the jump easier.
  • And go low on step in to explode into jump
  • 3 steps for all jumping kicks: 
  • bend knees, 
  • explode into jump,
  • tuck and spin.
  • Jump then spin don't spin then jump! Jumping is the most important.
