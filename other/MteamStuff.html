Front kick Flexibility
-2 min each side front and back splits (keep back straight)
-wall with partner. Lean up against wall, keep 45 deg with chest. have partner hold leg above shoulder while holding hand of person. 2 min each side.
-Go back and forth along floor doing position 1 then down.
-Then do low kicks (hip level) emphasizing form.
-Go across the floor with this drill: Go down on one knee, then explode into position 1. Helps with keeping back leg straight.

Side kick flexibility
-50 leg raises on wall (small motions) each side
-Slow wall kicks 25 each side
-Hold each leg out at max height 10 sec
-Partner drill: touch wall with hand. Leg on partner's shoulder. Don't stick butt out. 1 min each side.

-sit like Elliot with legs bent and apart and chest on floor

Day 2 General Notes

-PRESETS !!!!!! Do good on makki preset all the way out not in. 45 deg

-On front kick after high block (taeguk 1), Keep hands in for kick. Then do preset after you bend kicking leg. Then punch and bring negative hand back with POWER

-Arm all the way out on downblock (straight, no bend at elbow)

-triple or quadruple front kick: First one is lift. Also practice on ground with fast kicks. Do 10 reps to work on core while in the air.

-Front/side kicks: keep back leg straight looking forward back foot 180 towards wall

-Walking stance: legs straight

540s:

-drill where you don't kick and start on one leg like a flamingo.

-reverse thing. (Pulling shoulder back)

-WAIT to jump till you spot target. Then spot it again and land

-pop 360s with pulling bottom leg up will help work towards 540s and 720s
